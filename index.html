EzBolus — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Medical disclaimer

EzBolus is a calculator, not a medical device. It is not reviewed or approved by any regulator, and it does not know your medical history. Every dose it suggests is only as good as the numbers you enter and the ratios you configure.

Always use the ICR, ISF, and target glucose values given to you by your own care team — never guess. Review every suggested dose yourself before taking it, and never let this app replace your care team's guidance.

What EzBolus stores

Your dosing history, ratios, and settings are stored only on your device, in a local database and preference store. We do not operate any server that receives, stores, or has access to this data. Uninstalling the app removes it. The app also includes an in-app export/import feature so you can back up or move your own data yourself.

LibreLinkUp (optional)

If you choose to connect LibreLinkUp to pre-fill glucose readings, EzBolus talks directly to LibreView's own LibreLinkUp servers using your account credentials — the same servers the official LibreLinkUp app uses. We never see, receive, or route this traffic through any server we control.

  • Your password is used once to sign in and is never stored.
  • Your email and a session token are stored encrypted on your device (Android EncryptedSharedPreferences / Keystore) so you don't have to sign in every time.
  • Disconnecting in Settings clears this stored session immediately.

Notifications and permissions

EzBolus can optionally notify you when your insulin-on-board drops below a threshold you set. This runs entirely on your device using Android's notification and alarm scheduling APIs — no data leaves your device to make this work.

What we don't do

  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
  • No account creation or sign-in required to use the calculator.
  • No data is sold, shared, or transmitted to any third party we control.
